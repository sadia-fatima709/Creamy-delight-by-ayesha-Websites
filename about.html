📖 Our Story

Creamy Delight By Ayesha started in 2023 as a tiny home kitchen experiment. With just a whisk, a bowl, and an endless love for baking, Ayesha began creating desserts for family and friends. Word spread like wildfire — neighbors, friends of friends, and soon, complete strangers were knocking on her door for a taste of her magic.

Today, Creamy Delight is a beloved local brand known for its rich flavors, beautiful designs, and heartfelt service. But despite the growth, we remain true to our roots — every single dessert is still made in Ayesha's cozy home kitchen, with the same love and care as day one.

From classic chocolate cakes to exotic fruit tarts, from elegant wedding tiers to fun-themed cupcakes — Ayesha does it all with a smile and a sprinkle of magic. ✨
