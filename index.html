🍰 Our Sweet Story

Welcome to Creamy Delight By Ayesha — a home-based bakery where every dessert is a masterpiece. Founded with a passion for baking, we specialize in creating mouth-watering cakes, cupcakes, cookies, and custom desserts that bring joy to every celebration.

Whether it's a birthday, wedding, anniversary, Eid, or just a craving for something sweet, Ayesha's creations are designed to make your moments unforgettable. We use only the freshest ingredients, organic flavors, and zero preservatives — because you deserve the best!
From Eid celebrations to quiet Sunday afternoons, from grand weddings to simple "just because" moments — Ayesha pours her heart into every swirl of frosting, every sprinkle of decoration, and every carefully crafted detail. We believe that the best desserts are made not with machines, but with patience, passion, and a whole lot of love. 💕

Using only the freshest organic ingredients, zero preservatives, and time-honored recipes passed down through generations, we create desserts that don't just taste divine — they feel like home.
